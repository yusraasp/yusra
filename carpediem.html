INTRODUCTION

  • The majestic fully-custom built CARPE DIEM by Trinity Yachts is a 191ft (58.2m) luxury charter yacht embodying Hollywood glamour and exceptional amenities to suit her 12 charter guests.
  • CARPE DIEM'S styling was the work of esteemed designer Carol Williamson, who has drawn on European Art Deco themes.
  • The owner's wonderful collection of vintage black and white photos completes the glamorous ambience.
  • CARPE DIEM is well set-up for guests entertainment and communications with comprehensive audio-visual systems, satellite television, Apple TV, Xbox 360, and wi-fi..
