AZZAM



INTRODUCTION

  • Azzam (English: Resolute) is a private super yacht built by German shipyard Lürssen Yachts.
  • At 180 metres 590 ft in length, it is the longest private motor yacht in the world.
  • It has a beam of 20.8 metres 68 ft and an unusually shallow draft of 4.3 m (14 ft 1 in).
  • Azzam was launched on 5 April 2013.The yacht was delivered on 9 September 2013.
