ANDIAMO



INTRODUCTION

  • ANDIAMO is a superb 92ft (28m) American-built luxury charter yacht that offers great spaces for entertaining or relaxing for up to six charter guests in complete comfort.
  • ANDIAMO is the perfect adventure yacht, equipped with all the gear for hours of fun while on charter.
  • ANDIAMO provides a 13ft Highfield tender.
  • The professional crew of three are energetic and experienced, with unrivalled local knowledge to show charter guests a memorable vacation aboard ANDIAMO.
