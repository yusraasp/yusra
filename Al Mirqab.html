AL MIRQAB



INTRODUCTION

  • Al Mirqab is owned by Qatar's former Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Hamad bin Jassim bin Jaber Al Thani.
  • The yacht was built at Peters Schiffbau Wewelsfleth yard in Germany.
  • The yacht's architect was Tim Heywood, while the interior design was done by Andrew Winch Designs.
  • Her interior is styled by British designer designhouse Winch Design.
